The Universe

The vast uncomprehensibly large thing we all live in

Here's a time line of our Universe

  • 13.8 billion years ago - The Big Bang beginning of space and time
  • 13.65 billion years ago - Formation of the oldest known star
  • 13.5 billion years ago - First black holes form
  • 13.2 billion years ago - First Galaxies form
  • 12.6 billion years ago - The Formation of our Milky Way galaxy
  • 10.4 billion years ago - Universe becomes Habitable, first star systems emerge that are habitable for life
  • 9.7 billion years ago - The Universe reaches one third of it current diameter
  • 8.8 billion years ago - Formation of the Milky Way galaxies disk begins
  • 6.9 billion years ago - The Universe reaches one half of the size of the current Universe
  • 6.5 billion years ago - Milky Way galaxy starts to spiralizing to become a spiral galaxy it is today
  • 6 billion years ago - Dark energy overtakes gravity and the Universes expansion rate begins to accelerate
  • 5.5 billion years ago - Milky Way galaxy becomes a spiral galaxy
  • 4.57 billion years ago - The sun forms
  • 4.56 billion years ago - Prototype Earth forms
  • 4.53 billion years ago - Formation of the moon from the impact of Theia on Earth causing debris that came together to become the moon
  • 4.4 billion years ago - Earth's oceans begin to form from water brought by aesteroids
  • 4.2 billion years ago - The emergence of life on Earth
  • 2.5 billion years ago - Oxygenation of Earth by bacterially produced oxygen starting to occumulate in the atmosphere
  • 2.5 billion years ago - Movement of Earths tectonic plates begins
  • 1.8 billion years ago - Earths supercontinent Columbia forms
  • 1.5 billion years ago - Earths supercontinent Columbia breaks apart
  • 0.45 billion years ago - Plants and arthropods begin to colonize the Earths land
  • 0.42 billion years ago - Earths first air-breathing animals appear
  • 0.38 billion years ago - Earths first tree like plants begin to appear on land
  • 0.37 billion years ago - Late Devonian extinction occurs where 70% of all Earths species go extinct
  • 0.32-0.33 billion years ago - FIrst amniotes and first reptiles appear on Earth
  • 0.305 billion years ago - Earths atmospheric oxygen levels peak at 35% causing Arthropods to grow to unprecedented sizes
  • 0.3 billion years ago - Earths last supercontinent Pangea forms
  • 0.251 billion years ago - The Permian extinction event, Earths largest extinction event in history occured, where 90-96% of all species went extinct
  • 0.25 billion years ago - The brightest star in our night sky Sirius formed
  • 0.231-0.225 billion years ago - The first dinosaurs and mammals appeared on Earth
  • 0.201 billion years ago - Triassic extinction event occured on Earth where 75% of all species went extinct
  • 0.18 billion years ago - Earth supercontinent Pangea splits into Laurasia and Gondwana
  • 0.155 billion years ago - First Birds appeared on Earth
  • 0.1 billion years ago - First Bee's appear on Earth
  • 0.09-0.08 billion years ago - Indian subcontinent splits from Gondwana and Australia splits from Antartica
  • 0.066 billion years ago - Cretaceous extinction event occurs on Earth where 75% of all specis go extinct, including all non avian dinosaurs
  • 0.055-0.047 billion years ago - First modern birds appear, Indian subcontinent collides with Asia, and whales return to the oceans
  • 8-7.5 million years ago - Rigel and Betelgeuse stars form which are the first and second brightest stars in Orion respectively
  • 6 million years ago - Last common ancestors between humans and chimpanzess
  • 2.5 million years ago - Current ice age began on Earth
  • 2.2 million years ago - First memeber of the genus Homo appear on Earth
  • 0.78 million years ago - Last reversal of Earth's megnetic field
  • 0.5 million years ago - Earliest human engravings
  • 0.25 million years ago - Neanderthals evolve
  • 0.195 million years ago - Anatomically modern humans appear in Africa
  • 0.006 million years ago to present - Human civilization, which is the span of recorded history
